About

I studied as an electrical and electronic degree student. I am competent with microcontroller, signal and image processing. At my early career, I use C/C++, VHDL and Java. Later, I expand my skill with PHP, Java EE and Python to take the advantage of cloud ecosystems like Amazon Cloud and RedHat Openshift.

My primary interest is Internet of Things (IoT). I strive to build a seamless ecosystem of hardware and software that it is easy to use. Contact me if you are interested to build an embedded system or to design a web application.
